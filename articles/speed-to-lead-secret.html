The 5-Minute Rule

Industry data is brutal. If you respond to a lead within 5 minutes, you are 21 times more likely to qualify them than if you wait 30 minutes. After an hour, the lead is essentially cold.

The big national maintenance firms know this. That's why they have call centers staffed with people ready to jump on every enquiry. But as a local trade business owner, you have a problem: You are on the roof.

The Roofer's Dilemma

"You can't answer the phone safely while carrying a slate tile up a ladder. By the time you get down, the customer has already booked someone else."

For years, this meant small, high-quality firms lost out to bigger, less personal companies simply because they were slower. But technology has finally leveled the playing field.

How Automation Changes the Game

We use a platform called n8n to build workflows that act as your 24/7 digital secretary. Here is how the "Secret" works for our clients:

  1. Instant Capture: Whether the lead comes from your website, Facebook, or email, our system catches it instantly.
  2. AI Response: Within 45 seconds, an AI agent sends a personalized SMS or WhatsApp message. It sounds like you, but you didn't type it.
  3. Smart Qualification: The AI asks the right questions (Postcode? Type of roof?).
  4. Auto-Booking: If the lead is good, the AI offers slots directly from your Google Calendar.

The Result?

You come down from the roof at 4 PM and check your phone. Instead of 5 missed calls and voicemails to chase, you see 2 confirmed site visits booked for tomorrow morning. You didn't do a thing, but you beat every competitor to the punch.
